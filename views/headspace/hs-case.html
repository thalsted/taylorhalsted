Headspace Feature Proposal
how to improve user retention with crowdsourced sentiment data

User Research

In order to design the right solution, you need to know what problem you're solving... and whose problem it is. For this project, I built a survey to understand users' motivations, aspirations, challenges and pain points. Using a Facebook ad campaign, I targeted people interested in meditation to boost feedback, then loaded survey data for analysis.

User Analysis

Analysis showed that users who meditate more regularly find it easier to maintain the habit. Isolating reasons that users struggle would help to inform stronger features to promote routine. By distilling responses into composite dimensions, I found that respondents fell into one of three groups. Understanding the characteristics of each group allowed me to build personas that would become the foundation for driving higher retention.

Wireframe Concepts

As the glue between designers, developers, and management - it's important to establish a shared vision. Wireframing lets stakeholders visualize success, but more importantly, it provides an opportunity to contribute by facilitating a discussion. Driving alignment beforehand ensures less re-work and higher quality once development begins.

Build Product Roadmap

Envisioning a phased product launch can be tough, especially when it involves multiple functional groups and stakeholders. Roadmapping is critical to building consensus, attaining budget, and communicating progress to management. Documenting our goals, timeframe, and interdependencies made leading a cross-functional team, from concept to on-schedule launch, a seamless process.
